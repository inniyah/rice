RICE

Routines for Implementing C Expert systems

RICE is a fuzzy/multi-valued logic inference engine implemented as a C library. A C++ front-end by means of a wrapping class is provided. The library has been tested with Borland C/C++, Microsoft C/C++ and GNU C/C++. Documentation is available in WordPerfect 5.1 format (sorry ;-) or PostScript. RICE was used for some of the experiments I did during my research in the field of fuzzy logic in control.

The latest version of RICE (although old ;-) is available directly from this site. A complete distribution of RICE can also be obtained (this may be not the latest!) from various sites which are all accessible thru the CMU Artificial Intelligence Repository. Just search for fuzzy and you'll get there, or you can go directly to the RICE page of the repository.
